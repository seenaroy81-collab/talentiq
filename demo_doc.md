### **1. PLATFORM: Talent-IQ**
- **Technical**: AI-native recruitment orchestration engine.
- 🧸 **Simple**: A Super Smart Robot Recruiter.

### **2. FLOW: Process**
- **Technical**: End-to-end automated interview lifecycle.
- 🧸 **Simple**: Everything from reading your book to giving you a score.

### **3. INTERFACE: React**
- **Technical**: Component-based UI library for SPA states.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The pretty face and buttons you see and touch.

### **4. BRAIN: Node.js**
- **Technical**: Asynchronous server-side JavaScript runtime.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The boss who tells everyone what to do.

### **5. MIND: Llama 3.3**
- **Technical**: 70B parameter Large Language Model reasoning.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The smart thinking part that answers your questions.

### **6. EAR: Whisper-v3**
- **Technical**: ASR audio-to-text transformer processing.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The magic ear that hears you and turns voice into words.

### **7. SPEED: Groq**
- **Technical**: Low-latency LPU hardware inference.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The turbo boost that makes AI talk super fast!

### **8. LIBRARY: MongoDB**
- **Technical**: Document-based BSON data persistence.
- 🧸 **Simple**: A giant bookshelf where we keep all your information.

### **9. MEMORY: Redis**
- **Technical**: In-memory key-value session caching.
- 🧸 **Simple**: A sticky note for things we need to remember RIGHT NOW.

### **10. BOX: Docker**
- **Technical**: Containerized environment for isolated deployment.
- 🧸 **Simple**: A special box that keeps the code safe and ready to play.

### **11. SECURITY: Clerk**
- **Technical**: Managed JWT-based identity/auth provider.
- 🧸 **Simple**: The guard at the door who checks your ID.

### **12. ASSISTANT: Inngest**
- **Technical**: Event-driven asynchronous task pipeline.
- 🧸 **Simple**: A helper who sends emails for you while you sleep.

---

### **FULL INTERVIEW FLOW**
1. **READ**: Robot extracts text from your PDF.
2. **HEAR**: Whisper turns your voice into computer words.
3. **THINK**: Llama decides the best technical answer.
4. **TALK**: Robot speaks back to you with the next question.
5. **CODE**: AI reviews your logic and gives you hints.
6. **REPORT**: System writes a final gold-star score sheet.
